Welcome to use this map!

Here are some tips about the map.

* When you do not select any filter criteria, the map will display all MSOAs for you.
* PTAL（The Public Transport Accessibility Level） is a measurement of the public transport network accessibility. A higher score means the area has better public transportation conditions.
*You can choose a different school in the drop-down bar, and the value of distance will be calculated based on the school you choose. The method of calculating distance is from school coordinates to the centre point of MSOA.
* The rent and crime rate data are the average data of each MSOA.
* Now start and enjoy using the map!
